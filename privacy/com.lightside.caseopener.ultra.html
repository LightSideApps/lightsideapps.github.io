PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Case Opener Ultra (“Application”) for mobile devices that was created by Mobiray Software. The Application is a simulator of the opening cases with different kinds of weapon. Users can open boxes for some game money. They can earn game currency by trading or by watching ads. Also users can share their game guns in different social networks. Users have accounts with their information. We use Firebase for storage.

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

Personal information

We collect and associate with your account information like email, name, phone number, profile photo from social networks which you used for registration. The Application obtains the information you provide when you register in the Application. Please keep in mind that you will not be able to use the Application without registration.

Your personal information is secure in Firebase storage. We use Firebase for implementation authorization and storage your data.

Automatically Collected Information

The Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.

It’s necessary for stats and improving the Application.

The Application asks access to location. Why?

This Application asks permission for using approximate location information (not strict). It’s optional and you can denied this permissions or change your decision in settings (for Android 6 and higher). We ask your approximate location because it helps to show relevant ads for you.

The Application asks access to external storage. Why?

The Application asks access to external storage for cache video ads in memory device. It’s optional and you can deny access (for Android 6 and higher).

The Application has access to Internet connection. Why?

The Application asks access to Internet connection for showing ads.

Also we ask access to billing information because users can make in-app purchases.
